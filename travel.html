Getting to Bengaluru

A quick guide for friends and family flying in for November 10–12, 2026. Plan ahead — flights are long and visas take time. Tell us where you're setting off from and the routes, times, and money tips below will tailor to your trip.

Travelling from?

Start early. Sort your visa and flights well ahead, and leave a day to shake off the jet lag.

🕐 Time difference

Bengaluru is 13½ hours ahead of California (Pacific Standard Time in November). When it's 9:00 AM in Los Angeles, it's 10:30 PM in Bengaluru — give yourself a couple of days to reset.

Bengaluru is 10½ hours ahead of New York (Eastern Standard Time in November). When it's 9:00 AM in New York, it's 7:30 PM in Bengaluru.

Bengaluru is 5½ hours ahead of the UK (GMT in November). When it's 9:00 AM in Edinburgh, it's 2:30 PM in Bengaluru.

✈️ Flights

  • Fly into Kempegowda International Airport (BLR) in Devanahalli, about 40 km / 1–2 hours north of the city centre (traffic-dependent).
  • From California, plan on ~22–26 hours door to door. Good routings connect through a European hub (Frankfurt, Amsterdam, London, Paris), a Gulf hub (Dubai, Doha, Abu Dhabi), or Singapore (Singapore Airlines). San Francisco also has a nonstop to Bengaluru on Air India worth pricing out.
  • From New York, plan on ~18–22 hours door to door. Good routings connect through a European hub (Frankfurt, Amsterdam, London, Paris) or a Gulf hub (Dubai, Doha, Abu Dhabi). Air India also flies nonstop to Delhi or Mumbai with a connection to BLR.
  • From Edinburgh, plan on roughly 13–16 hours with one stop. Connect through London Heathrow onto a nonstop to BLR, or via a Gulf hub: Doha (Qatar) and Dubai (Emirates) both fly direct from Edinburgh.
  • Pre-arrange an airport pickup through your hotel, or use the Uber/Ola pickup zone. Keep your hotel address handy in writing.

📱 Phone & Internet

  • Easiest option: an eSIM (e.g. Airalo) you activate before landing — data works the moment you arrive.
  • A local SIM (Airtel or Jio) is cheap but needs ID/passport paperwork and can take a little time to activate. Counters are available at the airport.
  • WhatsApp is the default for messaging and calls in India; download it before you go.

🚗 Getting Around

  • We strongly recommend Uber & Ola (Ola is the local rideshare app) — by far the easiest and safest way to get around. Book in-app so the fare and route are set, with your hotel address ready for the driver.
  • Please don't plan to drive yourself. Traffic is intense and fast-moving — a rideshare or a hired car-and-driver is cheaper and far less stressful.
  • Traffic is heavy — budget extra time for anything across town, especially at rush hour.
