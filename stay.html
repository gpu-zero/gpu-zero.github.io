📍 Which area to pick

The venue is in [VENUE AREA]. Because city traffic is heavy, staying close to the venue (or to the events you'll attend most) saves a lot of time. A few popular areas:

  • Central / MG Road & Cubbon Park — well-connected, walkable, lots of hotels and dining.
  • Indiranagar — lively, great restaurants, cafés, and nightlife.
  • Koramangala — trendy and central-ish, lots to eat and do.
  • Whitefield — east side near the tech parks; convenient if your events are out east.
  • Near the airport (Devanahalli) — handy for a quick first night or a very early/late flight, but far from the city.

💸 Budget & apartments $

  • Treebo and FabHotels — reliable budget chains (read recent reviews).
  • Serviced apartments — great for families or longer stays (look on Booking.com / Airbnb).
  • Airbnb — plenty of options in Indiranagar, Koramangala, and HSR Layout.

✅ Booking tips

  • Book early — rooms near the venue go quickly during wedding season.
  • Ask whether the hotel offers airport pickup and confirm the cost.
  • Check the real distance and drive time to the venue, not just the map distance — traffic changes everything here.
  • Confirm breakfast, Wi-Fi, and AC are included, and note check-in time for early-morning arrivals.
