Explore Bengaluru

Bengaluru is green, easygoing, and famous for its gardens, food, and café culture. Here are our favourites — plus a few day trips if you want to make a holiday of it.

🌳 Things to do

  • Lalbagh Botanical Garden — historic gardens with a glasshouse and flower shows; lovely early morning.
  • Cubbon Park — leafy green heart of the city, great for a stroll.
  • Bangalore Palace — Tudor-style royal palace you can tour.
  • Vidhana Soudha — the grand state legislature building (admire the exterior, especially when lit up).
  • Tipu Sultan's Summer Palace & nearby Bull Temple (Dodda Basavana Gudi).
  • ISKCON Temple — striking hilltop Krishna temple.
  • Bannerghatta Biological Park — zoo and safari, ~1.5 hrs out.
  • HAL Aerospace Museum — fun for aviation buffs.
  • Microbreweries — Bengaluru is India's craft-beer capital, with many brewpubs to explore across the city.

🍽️ What to eat

Crisp masala dosa

  • South Indian tiffin — dosa, idli, and vada to start the day.
  • Filter coffee — the local ritual; strong and frothy, served in a brass tumbler.
  • Donne biryani — Bengaluru-style biryani served in a leaf bowl.
  • VV Puram Food Street — an evening street-food crawl.

Mostly vegetarian-friendly — South India is a paradise for veg eaters.

🥘 Where to eat

Indiranagar & Koramangala hold the densest cluster of modern restaurants, cafés, and bars — but some of the best meals are at the old-school institutions below. (Tap a name to open its map listing & website.)

  • MTR (Mavalli Tiffin Room) — open since 1924 and still the city's most iconic tiffin house; expect queues.
  • Kamath Bugle Rock — North Karnataka thali near Bull Temple.
  • Vidyarthi Bhavan — legendary benne (butter) masala dosa in Basavanagudi.
  • CTR / Shri Sagar — crisp benne dosa in Malleshwaram.
  • Brahmin's Coffee Bar — no-frills idli, vada & filter coffee.
  • Karavalli — refined coastal South Indian at The Gateway Hotel.

🛍️ Shopping

  • Commercial Street & Chickpet — bustling markets for clothes, jewellery, and silk sarees.
  • MG Road & Brigade Road — central shopping and people-watching.
  • UB City — upscale luxury mall.
  • Church Street — bookshops, cafés, and boutiques.

🚙 Day trips & getaways

  • Mysuru (Mysore) ~3 hrs — the magnificent Mysore Palace and Chamundi Hills; doable as a long day or an overnight.
  • Nandi Hills ~1.5 hrs — popular sunrise viewpoint north of the city.
  • Coorg (Kodagu) ~5–6 hrs — misty hills and coffee plantations; best as a weekend trip.
  • Shivanasamudra Falls ~3 hrs — dramatic waterfalls (best after the rains).
  • Hampi overnight — UNESCO World Heritage ruins; worth the extra travel if you have time.
  • Ramanagara ~1.5 hrs — granite hills made famous by the film Sholay.
