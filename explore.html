Explore Bengaluru

Bengaluru is green, easygoing, and famous for its gardens, food, and café culture. Here are our favorites — plus a few day trips if you want to make a holiday of it.

🌳 Things to do

  • Lalbagh Botanical Garden — historic gardens with a glasshouse and flower shows; lovely early morning.
  • Cubbon Park — leafy green heart of the city, great for a stroll.
  • Bangalore Palace — Tudor-style royal palace you can tour.
  • Vidhana Soudha — the grand state legislature building (admire the exterior, especially when lit up).
  • Tipu Sultan's Summer Palace & nearby Bull Temple (Dodda Basavana Gudi).
  • ISKCON Temple — striking hilltop Krishna temple.
  • Bannerghatta Biological Park — zoo and safari, ~1.5 hrs out.
  • HAL Aerospace Museum — fun for aviation buffs.
  • Microbreweries — Bengaluru is India's craft-beer capital: Toit, Arbor Brewing, Byg Brewski, and many more.

🍽️ Where to eat

  • South Indian classics — start your day with dosa, idli, and vada: MTR (Mavalli Tiffin Room), Vidyarthi Bhavan (legendary benne masala dosa), CTR / Shri Sagar, and Brahmin's Coffee Bar.
  • Filter coffee — the local ritual; have it strong and frothy.
  • Donne biryani — Bangalore-style biryani served in a leaf bowl.
  • VV Puram Food Street — evening street-food crawl.
  • Indiranagar & Koramangala — modern restaurants, cafés, and bars for dinner and drinks.

Mostly vegetarian-friendly — South India is a paradise for veg eaters.

🛍️ Shopping

  • Commercial Street & Chickpet — bustling markets for clothes, jewelry, and silk sarees.
  • MG Road & Brigade Road — central shopping and people-watching.
  • UB City — upscale luxury mall.
  • Church Street — bookshops, cafés, and boutiques.

🚙 Day trips & getaways

  • Mysuru (Mysore) ~3 hrs — the magnificent Mysore Palace and Chamundi Hills; doable as a long day or an overnight.
  • Nandi Hills ~1.5 hrs — popular sunrise viewpoint north of the city.
  • Coorg (Kodagu) ~5–6 hrs — misty hills and coffee plantations; best as a weekend trip.
  • Shivanasamudra Falls ~3 hrs — dramatic waterfalls (best after the rains).
  • Hampi overnight — UNESCO World Heritage ruins; worth the extra travel if you have time.
  • Ramanagara ~1.5 hrs — granite hills made famous by the film Sholay.
Good to know: opening hours, ticket prices, and availability change often — please double-check before you set out, and book popular day trips ahead. Happy to give more tailored suggestions — just ask us!
