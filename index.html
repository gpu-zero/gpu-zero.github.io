The Celebrations

Our wedding unfolds over several days. Here's how the celebrations come together — join us for as much as you can.

  • Mehendi (women only)iA mehendi is a joyful pre-wedding gathering where henna designs are applied to the bride's and guests' hands and feet. This event is for women only. Tue, Nov 10 · 3:30 PM Ajay's Parents' Home
  • Vara Pooje & ReceptioniThe vara pooje is a Kannada ceremony in which the bride's family formally welcomes and honours the groom (the vara) the evening before the wedding — followed by a reception to celebrate together. Wed, Nov 11 · 6:00 PM Convention hall Dinner served 8:00 PM
  • Wedding Ceremony Thu, Nov 12 · 10:00 AM Convention hall Lunch served 2:00 PM

What to Wear

Indian weddings are a celebration of colour, so come dressed to join in. Both traditional Indian wear and Western wear are warmly welcome. A few notes to help:

  • Please avoid all-black or all-white outfits — they're not quite right for a wedding celebration.
  • The ceremony is shoes-and-socks-off, so leave the heels at home and pick footwear that's easy to slip on and off.
  • For women, we suggest knee-length or longer dresses or skirts. Bengaluru in November is warm by day, so natural fabrics like linen and cotton will keep you comfortable.
